What is MAYA?

Autodesk Maya is a professional 3D computer graphics software used worldwide for creating realistic animations, 3D models, simulations, visual effects, and rendering. It is widely adopted in industries like film, gaming, and architecture due to its powerful tools and flexibility.

Capabilities of MAYA

  • Advanced 3D Modeling (polygonal, NURBS, and subdivision surfaces)
  • Professional-grade Animation Tools with rigging and keyframe systems
  • Dynamics & Effects (particles, fluids, hair, cloth, and physics simulations)
  • Lighting & Rendering with Arnold Renderer
  • Supports game development and integration with engines like Unity/Unreal
