What is 3D Animation?

3D Animation is the art of creating moving images in a digital three-dimensional space. It is widely used in films, games, advertising, and visual storytelling. By animating models, characters, and environments, I can transform static designs into engaging experiences.

Capabilities

  • Character animation (walk cycles, expressions, action)
  • Environmental animation (camera fly-throughs, effects)
  • Physics-based simulation (cloth, particles, fluids)
  • Integration with VFX for cinematic storytelling
  • Exporting animations for games, films, and AR/VR
