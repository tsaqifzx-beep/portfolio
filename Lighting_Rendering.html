What is Lighting & Rendering?

Lighting & Rendering are essential stages in 3D animation and visual design. Lighting brings mood, depth, and atmosphere to a scene, while Rendering converts 3D models into final 2D images or animations. Together, they transform raw models into stunning visuals that feel alive and immersive.

Capabilities

  • Realistic lighting setups (day, night, studio, cinematic)
  • Material and texture adjustments for lifelike results
  • Advanced rendering using ray tracing & global illumination
  • Creating dramatic shadows and highlights for storytelling
  • Rendering for games, movies, and animation projects
